Harbour at Lakewood

The Harbour Townhomes at Lakewood offer luxurious lake front living in the heart of the 40502 zip code. As soon as you walk into these gorgeous two story units, you are greeted by incredible views of the Lexington lake reservoir, 10' foot ceilings, engineered hardwood floors, private outdoor living patios, a gourmet kitchen and elegant trim details. As an owner, your family gets to enjoy tremendous privacy in this gated community and a low maintenance lifestyle as the HOA takes care of all landscaping and snow removal. You also have access to a private dock and pontoon boat for fishing and relaxing with friends and family.

Patchen Wilkes

The Patchen Wilkes Townhomes are conveniently located on Winchester Road just a few minutes from the Hamburg Shopping Area and I-75. Upon completetion, Patchen Wilkes will be a vibrant community that includes 54 town homes, gorgeous landscaping, and a walking path along the Patchen Wilkes lake. Each townhome is unique and can be customized with finishes that meet your individual style.

Beaumount Park

We are incredibly proud of the community we built at Beaumont Park Townhomes. From 2013 to 2015 our team of builders, designers, and realtors delivered 74 townhomes to satisfied home owners, established a self governing HOA, and learned a great deal about constructing high quality townhomes. Our success at Beaumont shaped our company's vision and set the standard for all of our communities.
